About us

Louie is my inspiration for Clean Leash Company. Like most dog owners- I start my mornings with a long dog walk. A mandortay requirement of being a good dog parent is cleaning up after your dog. And every time after I cleaned up after him, I never felt clean- I cant explain it. Clean Leash gives you the opportunity to continue giving your dog the attention and time they need while still feeling clean

With Clean Leash, every walk becomes a chance to reset—not just for your dog, but for you too. We are about peace of mind, confidence, and starting your day with a fresh perspective. Once you try it, you'll wonder how you ever lived without it.
